## Long Description
> **Partner Center → Description** (≤ 10,000 characters)

```
FocusFlow — Deep Focus & App Blocker for Windows

Take back control of your attention. FocusFlow is the most powerful focus and productivity tool for Windows — it doesn't just suggest you focus, it enforces it. Block distracting apps at the process level, lock your desktop into a distraction-free workspace, and build lasting productive habits.

Whether you're a student, developer, writer, or anyone who struggles with distractions, FocusFlow gives you the tools to do your best work — every day.

──────────────────────────────────────
🔒 REAL APP BLOCKING — NOT JUST REMINDERS
──────────────────────────────────────
FocusFlow monitors running Windows processes and closes blocked apps the instant they open. Not browser tabs — actual applications. Block Chrome, Discord, Steam, Spotify, or any executable you choose.

• Block any app by process name
• Always-on blocks, scheduled time windows, or session-only blocking
• Daily allowances — give an app a time budget (e.g. 30 min of YouTube per day)
• Website blocker — block domains at the hosts file level, across all browsers
• Keyword blocker — block any window whose title contains words you choose

──────────────────────────────────────
🖥 FOCUS LAUNCHER — KIOSK MODE FOR DEEP WORK
──────────────────────────────────────
The Focus Launcher turns your entire desktop into a focused workspace the moment a session begins:

• Hides the Windows taskbar — no more "just one quick check" of notifications
• Activates Nuclear Mode automatically for maximum blocking from second one
• PIN-gated breaks — every break requires your session PIN to prevent impulsive exits
• Hard Lock mode — no breaks at all, pure deep work until the timer ends
• Crash-safe — your taskbar is always restored even if the app crashes

──────────────────────────────────────
⏱ FOCUS SESSIONS & POMODORO TIMER
──────────────────────────────────────
• Timed focus sessions: 15, 25, 45, 60, or 90 minutes — or set a custom duration
• Pomodoro mode — automatic work/break cycles with short and long breaks
• Session PIN — lock yourself in so you can't end a session early
• Live countdown in the taskbar title so you always know your remaining time
• Sound aversion — an alert tone plays each time you try to open a blocked app

──────────────────────────────────────
☢ NUCLEAR MODE — MAXIMUM ENFORCEMENT
──────────────────────────────────────
When you need zero escape routes, Nuclear Mode blocks over 30 system utilities — including Task Manager, PowerShell, the Run dialog, and browser developer consoles — so there is no easy way out. You chose to focus. Nuclear Mode makes that choice stick.

──────────────────────────────────────
📊 STATS, REPORTS & INSIGHTS
──────────────────────────────────────
• Focus dashboard — daily streak, tasks completed, blocked app attempts, focus score
• Weekly report — delivered every Sunday with your peak hours and best days
• Temptation log — every blocked attempt logged with timestamp and app name
• Session history — every session stored locally with duration and outcome
• Productivity trends and recommendations based on your own patterns

──────────────────────────────────────
✅ TASKS, HABITS & NOTES
──────────────────────────────────────
• Schedule tasks with time slots, durations, and priority levels
• Recurring tasks — daily, weekdays, weekly
• Daily habit tracker with completion streaks
• Session notes and daily logs — everything stored locally on your PC

──────────────────────────────────────
🔐 100% PRIVATE — YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES YOUR PC
──────────────────────────────────────
• No account required — install and use immediately
• No internet connection needed — all features work completely offline
• No telemetry, analytics, or usage data sent anywhere
• All data stored locally in a SQLite database at %USERPROFILE%\.focusflow\
• Automatic rolling backups and local crash logs only

──────────────────────────────────────
⚙ SYSTEM & CONVENIENCE
──────────────────────────────────────
• Runs quietly in the system tray — right-click to restore, toggle blocking, or quit
• Auto-start with Windows (optional, no admin required)
• Windows toast and balloon notifications for sessions, blocks, and weekly reports
• Built for Windows 10 and Windows 11

──────────────────────────────────────
WHO IS FOCUSFLOW FOR?
──────────────────────────────────────
• Students who need to stay off social media during study sessions
• Developers, writers, and designers who do deep work
• People with ADHD who need hard commitment devices, not optional timers
• Anyone who has tried other focus apps and found they could just dismiss them

If you've ever told yourself "I'll just check quickly" and lost an hour — FocusFlow is for you.
```
